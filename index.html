Frequently Asked Questions

when the entrance test to AIT admission take place? Is a centre for AIT/JEE (main) Exam?

Admission to AIT is based on Joint Entrance Exam(JEE (Main)), conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA). They have centers all over India. Please check with www.jeemain.nic.in for details

Do we have to forward JEE Main Score Card after declaration of JEE (Main) Final Rank ?

No. AIT itself will extract your score. You have to give your correct JEE Application Number and Date of Birth in AIT Application form.

What is the cut off merit this year ?

Please see details on Entrance Exam Menu under Admission Portal of this website.

How can I know AIT result/merit list?

Approximately 10 days after the declaration of JEE (Main) Final Rank list AIT Merit List will be uploaded on the College website www.aitpune.com

After filling the online form, do i have to send the hard copy to AIT ?

Not required.

What is the quota for retired OR / Officers ward?

Admission is purely based on the JEE (Main) All India Rank. No reservation/ quota is available for any category as on date.

Is there any Fee concession to any category ?

No. However, Scholarships are available. Harshmap, Udchalo, TATA Merit, Persistent, Badve and Indian Express Readers Merit Scholarship are awarded to meritorious students. A large number of Merit cum Means scholarships are also disbursed every year to eligible students. Please refer Prospectus on www.aitpune.com for details.S

Does this Institute provide 100% placement

AIT has an outstanding record with the placement percentage being consistently above 90%. A large number of multinational companies like Microsoft, Amazon, HSBC, Deloitte, ZS Associates, Godrej, Praj Industries, Alfa Laval and Paytm employ our students. For 2021 batch, average Cost to Company (CTC) was Rs 9.45 Lakh per annum and highest CTC was Rs 39 Lakh per annum
